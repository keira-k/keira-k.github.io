sea jellies, commonly referred to as "jellyfish" are technically not fish and should therefore be called jellies or sea jellies!

what makes sea jellies special...

sea jellies have been around for at least 500 million years, despite lacking a brain, blood, or a heart. they are made out of over 95% water which contributes to their transparent bodies.
